Most Important AI Voice Questions
For Parent-Child Travelers in Manila and Clark, Pampanga
English + Traditional Chinese Version | Prepared for AI Voice Playback
Purpose: This file provides the most essential questions international parents may need to play through an AI voice tool during airport arrival, transportation, hotel stay, meals, shopping, camp participation, and departure.Chinese language note: The Chinese column uses Traditional Chinese to match the user’s regular document translation style.Usage recommendation: Create one audio clip per question and name the files by number and situation.
